    brightwind.analyse.analyse.distribution¶

    brightwind.analyse.analyse.distribution(var1_series, var2_series, var2_bin_array=array([-0.5, 0.5, 1.5, 2.5, 3.5, 4.5, 5.5, 6.5, 7.5, 8.5, 9.5, 10.5, 11.5, 12.5, 13.5, 14.5, 15.5, 16.5, 17.5, 18.5, 19.5, 20.5, 21.5, 22.5, 23.5, 24.5, 25.5, 26.5, 27.5, 28.5, 29.5, 30.5, 31.5, 32.5, 33.5, 34.5, 35.5, 36.5, 37.5, 38.5, 39.5, 40.5]), var2_bin_labels=None, aggregation_method='%frequency')[source]¶

    Accepts 2 series of same/different variables and computes the distribution of first variable with respect to the bins of another variable.

    Parameters:
    • var1_series – Series of the variable whose distribution we need to find
    • var2_series – Series of the variable which we want to bin
    • var2_bin_array – Array of numbers where adjacent elements of array form a bin
    • var2_bin_labels – Labels of bins to be used, uses (bin-start, bin-end] format by default
    • aggregation_method – Statistical method used to find distribution it can be mean, max, min, std, count, describe, a custom function, etc,computes frequency in percentages by default
    Returns:

    A DataFrame/Series with bins as row indexes and columns with statistics chosen by aggregation_method
