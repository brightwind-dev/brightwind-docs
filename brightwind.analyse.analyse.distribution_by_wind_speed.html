    brightwind.analyse.analyse.distribution_by_wind_speed¶

    brightwind.analyse.analyse.distribution_by_wind_speed(wdspd, return_data=False)[source]¶

    Accepts 2 series of same/different variables and computes the distribution of first variable with respect to the bins of another variable.

    Parameters:
    • wdspd – Series of the variable whose distribution we need to find
    • return_data (bool) – Set to True if you want the data returned.
